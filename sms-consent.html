SMS Consent Policy

How we obtain, manage, and honor your consent for text message communications from our firm.

Overview

Rountree Leitman Klein & Geer, LLC ("RLKG," "we," "us," or "our") uses text messaging via toll-free number (844) 907-4565 for internal communications among our partners and staff regarding phone referral intakes. This policy describes how we obtain consent before sending text messages and how recipients may opt out at any time.

Verbal Consent Script

When onboarding a new team member or referral partner who will receive text messages from our firm, we use the following verbal consent script:

Verbal Consent Script

"As part of our intake referral process at Rountree Leitman Klein & Geer, we use text messaging to communicate phone referral intake information among our partners and staff. Messages will be sent to your mobile number from (844) 907-4565. Message frequency will vary based on intake volume. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out of receiving text messages, or reply HELP for assistance. Do you consent to receiving these text messages?"

Consent is recorded by the staff member conducting the onboarding, including the name of the individual providing consent, the date and time consent was given, and the phone number to which messages will be sent.

Confirmation Message

After verbal consent is obtained, the recipient will receive a confirmation text message:

Confirmation Text

"RLKG Law: You have been opted in to receive text messages regarding phone referral intakes from Rountree Leitman Klein & Geer, LLC. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help."

Message Details

Sender: (844) 907-4565

Message content: Internal phone referral intake notifications among firm partners and staff

Frequency: Message frequency varies based on referral intake volume

Carriers: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message received from (844) 907-4565. Upon receipt of your STOP request, you will receive a final confirmation message and no further text messages will be sent to your number.

You may also opt out by contacting our office directly at (404) 584-1238 or by emailing us at info@rlkglaw.com.

Help & Support

For assistance with our text messaging program, reply HELP to any message or contact our office:

Phone: (404) 584-1238

Email: info@rlkglaw.com

Address: Century Plaza I, 2987 Clairmont Road, Suite 350, Atlanta, GA 30329

Privacy

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or any opt-in information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of employment or any business relationship with our firm. Consent is not shared across business entities or used for purposes other than those described in this policy.
